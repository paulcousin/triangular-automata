Elementary Cellular Automata

Under a certain condition, ETA rules maintain the uniformity of layers. This is the case when the 2nd and 3rd digits of the binary rule number are the same, as well as the 6th and 7th. $$ \Big(R(1)=R(2)\Big) \land \Big(R(5)=R(6)\Big) $$ 64 ETA rule meet this condition and they are all striclty equivallent to a unique symmetric Elementary Cellular Automaton (ECA) rule [18].
