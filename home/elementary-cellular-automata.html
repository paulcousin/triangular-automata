Elementary Cellular Automata

Under a certain condition, ETA rules maintain the uniformity of layers. This is the case when the $2^\text{nd}$ and $3^\text{rd}$ digits of the binary rule number are the same, as well as the $6^\text{th}$ and $7^\text{th}$. $$ \Big[R(1)=R(2)\Big] \land \Big[R(5)=R(6)\Big] $$ 64 ETA rules satisfy this condition and they are all striclty equivalent to a unique symmetric Elementary Cellular Automaton (ECA) [19].
